E-Library is an android app developed to help you with your studies. Its main objective is to make all the textbooks and reference books used in the academic courses available for free of cost to the students. It enables you to download the books from the app, store it in the device storage, open it in the app, and also delete the books at the end of the semester. All you require is an internet connection and enough storage to store the books in your phone. Feel free to give feedback, book suggestion, bug reports from the app. New books will be added on a regular basis. Click on download button now and make learning easy with the help of E-library.
  • Steps to install E-library app
  • 1. Download apk file from link above. When browser promts "This type of file may harm your device, download anyway?", tap "yes". Do not worry, it simply says so because android treats any apps downloaded from sources other than the playstore as "harmful apps".
  • 2. Go to the settings app on your device and navigate to security menu. In the security menu, make sure that unknown sources toggle is set to on. This allows the apk installer to install apks from sources other than the playstore.
  • 3. Click on the downloaded apk and install the app. If you face any problem while installing the app, please contact us at elib.feedback@gmail.com
E-Library exhibits very simple and user friendly UI. To navigate to a particular book, follow these steps: Department Semester Subject Book. Tap on Download button once it turns from grey to blue. Once download is complete, tap on Open button to read the book. To delete a book, tap on Delete button to delete the book from your device storage. You may give us a feedback, suggest a book, and report a bug from the app by tapping the menu on the top right corner of the home screen of the app. You may also update the app from the same menu.
